✨ From Quiet Devotion to Active Service ✨

At Reverend Marius' Shepherd's Chapel, worship does not end when the service does. From the deep well of our faith, we equip one another to become the hands and feet of service in the world. There is a place for everyone here — come as you are and join us in one of the gatherings below!

🔔 Weekly Gatherings 🔔

⛪ Sunday Worship

Sundays — 10:00 AM
Traditional, steadfast worship in the little white chapel on the hill.

📗 Wednesday Bible Study

Wednesdays — 7:00 PM
Grounding ourselves in timeless truth, one chapter at a time.

🤝 Ministries & Service 🤝

🌍 Community Outreach

Serving our neighbors through food drives, visits to the homebound, and seasonal service days. Turning quiet devotion into active community impact.

🎵 Music & Choir Ministry

Lifting our voices in praise. All who love to sing are welcome — rehearsals before Sunday service.

🤶 Fellowship & Coffee Hour

Stay after Sunday worship for coffee, treats, and warm conversation with the Chapel family.

👶 Youth & Children's Corner

A welcoming space for our youngest members to learn, play, and grow in faith.
