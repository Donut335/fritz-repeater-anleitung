Reverend Marius Schmitz

✨ My Unexpected Calling ✨

Some callings are nurtured over a lifetime, but mine arrived in a sudden flash of curiosity. I didn't spend years planning to lead a church; in fact, my journey began by simply typing a question into a search engine about how to become ordained. But what started as a casual search shifted everything. Within a matter of hours, a profound clarity washed over me. I realized this wasn't just a fleeting interest—it was my absolute passion and my true calling.

That sudden, undeniable conviction is the heartbeat of The Shepherd’s Chapel. It taught me that a life-changing purpose can reach us at any moment, and it is my greatest honor to guide a steadfast sanctuary where others can discover that same truth.

"I believe that a quiet, unchanging sanctuary provides the strongest possible foundation for an active life of faith."
— Reverend Marius Schmitz
